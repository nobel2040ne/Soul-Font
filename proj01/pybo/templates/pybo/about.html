About SoulFont

What is SoulFont?

SoulFont turns your handwriting into a real, installable font. Fill in a short template by hand, upload it, and an AI model reconstructs every Korean syllable in your style — then packages it as a TTF you can use anywhere.

How it works

  1. Download the handwriting template and write the sample characters.
  2. Upload the filled PDF. We crop and clean each cell automatically.
  3. A memory-augmented neural network generates the full character set from your few samples.
  4. Generated glyphs (plus your traced English & symbols) are assembled into a TTF, with your own font metadata.
